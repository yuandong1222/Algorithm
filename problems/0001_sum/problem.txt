Write a function to calculate a and b.
The input format will be two numbers in one line, e.g:
=============>
1 1
2 2
98 100
0 0
<=============
two zero in one line means the end.

The output should be the sum of two numbers in the line, e.g:
=============>
2
4
198
<=============
All numbers are less or equal than 32767.
